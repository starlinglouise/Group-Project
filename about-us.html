Louise

Louise has been a gin drinker for the past 10 years. She set herself the challenge to try 100 different gins before her 30th birthday (which she achieved ... not all on the same day!) She is currently closing in on 200. She also makes her own sloe gin and seeks out all the gin cocktails when she’s out and about! When she’s not drinking gin, she works and lives in London.

Mathilde

As any French person, Mathilde loves wine but she discovered herself a soft spot for gin when she moved to London over a year ago. Now she can’t imagine her life without this delicious beverage! A bit of gin, some tonic and a slice of lime and she’s in heaven. She almost considered to choose G&T as a favourite drink, but clichés are too strong and nothing can beat wine.
